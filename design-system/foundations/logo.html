Usage Guidelines

check_circle

Do

  • Use the logo in its original proportions
  • Ensure sufficient contrast with the background
  • Use the reversed version on dark backgrounds
  • Maintain the required clear space around the logo
error

Don't

  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
  • Change the logo colors outside approved variants
  • Place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Add effects such as drop shadows or outlines
