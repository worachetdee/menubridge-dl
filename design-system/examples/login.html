code Implementation Notes

  • Layout: Uses a flex container with items-center justify-center to vertically and horizontally center the login card.
  • Typography: Uses Epilogue for the "Log in" header to establish brand voice, while the form labels and inputs reuse Inter for legibility.
  • Inputs: Custom styled inputs with bg-[#f9fbfb] (off-white) to reduce contrast harshness against the white card.
  • Brand Header: The simple "MenuBridge" text logo is used instead of a full graphic to keep the loading weight low.
