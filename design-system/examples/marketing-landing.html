How it works

Turn your menu into a clear English menu in minutes

1

Upload or enter your menu

Take a photo, upload a file, or type your Japanese menu directly.

2

AI generates clear English descriptions

Each dish gets a guest-friendly explanation and dietary tags — not a literal translation, but a description that helps guests understand what they're ordering.

3

Share with guests via QR code

Publish a mobile-friendly bilingual menu. Place the QR code at your tables — guests scan and browse instantly.

Three numbered steps (upload, AI generates, share via QR) matching the pitch deck flow, each in a card with description.
